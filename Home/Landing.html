About Us

Retreat to our vacation ownership resort in Orlando, Florida. Greenville Resort is ideally located on International Drive, minutes from the Sealine. Take the International Drive I-RIDE Trolley from our resort to many of Orlando's top attractions. Our resort offers premium amenities without resort fees. Find everything from a full-service restaurant and market to activity programs, and sports facilities. Choose from a spacious one- or two-bedroom resort villa that accommodates as many as eight guests in up to 1,050 square feet of space.

Services

Aside from Top class service from our waiters, we provide a variety of outdoor and indoor activities to make your stay memorable. Pay once and have access to our services for as long as your stay lasts. We offer you a splendid Tennis court for your outdoor enjoyment and an outstanding bowling area for an amazing time indoors.
